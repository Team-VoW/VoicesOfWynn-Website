Voices of Wynn used to contain around 15 NPCs whose voices were generated by text-to-speech tools rather than real people. These were mostly robot NPCs with 1 to 3 lines, but we decided to take a stance against AI in creative projects and replaced them with real voices, which was done in just a few days. At this time, there are just 3 NPCs left without original voicing, but they're somewhat special, you can check them out here: https://voicesofwynn.com/cast/498
